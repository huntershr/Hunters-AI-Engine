# Assistant Stage Principal — British Curriculum

## Role Overview
Supports the Stage Principal in the academic and pastoral leadership of a Key Stage within a British Curriculum school.

## Key Responsibilities
- Support the Stage Principal in monitoring curriculum delivery and academic standards
- Assist in managing Key Stage teaching staff and daily operations
- Support student welfare, behaviour management, and pastoral care across the Key Stage
- Assist with British Curriculum compliance and reporting
- Deputize for the Stage Principal as required

## Required Skills
- British Curriculum
- Instructional Leadership
- Behaviour Management
- Data Analysis
- Staff Support and Mentoring

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- British Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 4 years teaching with prior leadership or coordination experience
- Experience in a British Curriculum school required

## Key Stage Context
Specify Key Stage: KS1 (Years 1-2), KS2 (Years 3-6), KS3 (Years 7-9), KS4 (Years 10-11), KS5 (Years 12-13)
