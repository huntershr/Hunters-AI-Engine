# Facilities Manager

## Key Responsibilities
- Oversee the management and maintenance of all physical facilities, buildings, and infrastructure across organizational sites
- Develop and implement preventive and reactive maintenance programs to ensure facilities are operational, safe, and compliant
- Manage facilities service contracts for cleaning, security, HVAC, electrical, and other technical services
- Coordinate space planning, fit-out projects, and office relocations in collaboration with relevant departments
- Manage the facilities budget including maintenance costs, utilities, and service contracts
- Ensure all facilities comply with health, safety, fire, and building regulations
- Supervise in-house facilities staff and external contractors and monitor service delivery performance

## Required Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Facilities Management, or Business Administration
- 6+ years of experience in facilities management across multi-site or large-scale operations
- Knowledge of building systems including HVAC, electrical, plumbing, and fire safety
- IFMA or equivalent facilities management certification preferred

## Core Competencies
- Multi-site facilities operations management
- Contractor and service vendor management
- Health, safety, and regulatory compliance
- Budget management and cost optimization
- Proactive maintenance planning and asset management
