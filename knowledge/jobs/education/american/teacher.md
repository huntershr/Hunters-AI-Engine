# Teacher — American Curriculum

## Role Overview
Delivers subject-specific lessons aligned to American Curriculum / Common Core standards. Reports to Principal or Department Head.

## Key Responsibilities
- Plan and deliver lessons aligned to American Curriculum / Common Core standards
- Assess students using standards-based grading
- Maintain grade book and progress reports per American system
- Participate in Parent-Teacher conferences
- Contribute to curriculum mapping by grade level

## Required Skills
- American Curriculum
- Lesson Planning
- Classroom Management
- Standards-Based Assessment
- Differentiated Instruction

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- American Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 2 years teaching in an American Curriculum school
- State teaching certification or equivalent preferred

## Grade Level Context
Specify grade level: KG, Grade 1-5 (Elementary), Grade 6-8 (Middle School), Grade 9-12 (High School)
