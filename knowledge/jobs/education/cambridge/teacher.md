# Teacher — Cambridge Curriculum

## Role Overview
Delivers subject-specific lessons aligned to Cambridge International standards. Reports to Head of Department or Cambridge Coordinator.

## Key Responsibilities
- Plan and deliver lessons aligned to Cambridge International curriculum
- Prepare students for Cambridge assessments (Checkpoint, IGCSE, A Level)
- Mark and moderate work to Cambridge assessment standards
- Attend Cambridge training and moderation sessions
- Contribute to Cambridge programme review

## Required Skills
- Cambridge Curriculum
- IGCSE
- Lesson Planning
- Classroom Management
- Exam Preparation

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- Cambridge Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 2 years teaching Cambridge curriculum
- Cambridge trained preferred

## Stage Context
Specify stage: Cambridge Primary, Cambridge Lower Secondary, Cambridge Upper Secondary (IGCSE), Cambridge Advanced (A Level)
