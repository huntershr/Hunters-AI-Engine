# Stage Supervisor / Nazir (Stage Principal) — Egyptian National Curriculum

## Role Overview
Provides academic and operational leadership for an educational stage (Primary, Preparatory, or Secondary) within an Egyptian National Curriculum school. Reports to the School Principal (Mudir).

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead curriculum delivery aligned to Egyptian Ministry of Education curriculum for the stage
- Manage and develop teaching staff within the stage
- Monitor student attainment and Ministry assessment outcomes across the stage
- Ensure compliance with Ministry of Education requirements and inspection readiness
- Represent the stage in Senior Leadership Team meetings and Ministry inspection visits

## Required Skills
- Egyptian National Curriculum
- Instructional Leadership
- Staff Performance Management
- Data Analysis
- Stakeholder Communication

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- Egyptian National Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 6 years teaching with at least 3 years in a middle or senior leadership role
- Experience leading a stage in an Egyptian national or language school required
