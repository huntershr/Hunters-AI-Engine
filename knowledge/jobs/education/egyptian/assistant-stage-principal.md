# Assistant Nazir (Assistant Stage Principal) — Egyptian National Curriculum

## Role Overview
Supports the Stage Supervisor (Nazir) in the academic and operational leadership of an educational stage within an Egyptian National Curriculum school.

## Key Responsibilities
- Support the Stage Supervisor in monitoring curriculum delivery and academic standards
- Assist in managing stage teaching staff and daily operations
- Support student welfare, behaviour management, and Ministry assessment preparation
- Assist with Ministry of Education compliance and inspection preparation
- Deputize for the Stage Supervisor as required

## Required Skills
- Egyptian National Curriculum
- Instructional Leadership
- Behaviour Management
- Data Analysis
- Staff Support and Mentoring

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- Egyptian National Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 4 years teaching with prior leadership or coordination experience
- Experience in an Egyptian national or language school required
