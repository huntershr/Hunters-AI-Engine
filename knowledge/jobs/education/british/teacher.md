# Teacher — British Curriculum

## Role Overview
Delivers subject-specific lessons aligned to the National Curriculum (England). Reports to Head of Department or Stage Principal.

## Key Responsibilities
- Plan and deliver lessons aligned to British Curriculum / National Curriculum (England)
- Assess student progress using formative and summative assessment
- Maintain records aligned to British reporting standards
- Contribute to Key Stage planning and schemes of work
- Participate in parents evenings and written reports

## Required Skills
- British Curriculum
- Lesson Planning
- Classroom Management
- Formative and Summative Assessment
- Differentiated Instruction

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- British Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 2 years teaching in a British Curriculum school
- QTS preferred or equivalent

## Key Stage Context
Specify Key Stage: KS1 (Years 1-2), KS2 (Years 3-6), KS3 (Years 7-9), KS4 (Years 10-11), KS5 (Years 12-13)
