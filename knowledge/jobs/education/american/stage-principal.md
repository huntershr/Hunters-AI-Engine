# Stage Principal (Elementary / Middle / High School Principal) — American Curriculum

## Role Overview
Provides academic and operational leadership for a school level (Elementary, Middle, or High School) within an American Curriculum school. Reports to the Head of School.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead curriculum delivery aligned to American Curriculum / Common Core standards for the school level
- Manage and develop Department Heads and teaching staff within the level
- Monitor student achievement data and standards-based progress across the level
- Ensure compliance with American accreditation and reporting requirements
- Represent the school level in Senior Leadership Team meetings and accreditation visits

## Required Skills
- American Curriculum
- Instructional Leadership
- Staff Performance Management
- Data Analysis
- Stakeholder Communication

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- American Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 6 years teaching with at least 3 years in a middle or senior leadership role
- Experience as a Principal or Assistant Principal in an American Curriculum school required

## Grade Level Context
Specify grade level: KG, Grade 1-5 (Elementary), Grade 6-8 (Middle School), Grade 9-12 (High School)
