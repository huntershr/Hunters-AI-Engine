# Teaching Assistant — Egyptian National Curriculum

## Role Overview
Supports the class teacher in delivering lessons and supporting individual students within an Egyptian National Curriculum school environment.

## Key Responsibilities
- Support class teacher in lesson delivery
- Work with individual students and small groups
- Prepare classroom materials and resources
- Support students with Special Educational Needs where required
- Maintain a safe and positive learning environment

## Required Skills
- Egyptian National Curriculum
- Classroom Support
- Special Educational Needs
- Child Safeguarding
- Parent Communication

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- Egyptian National Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 1 year experience as TA or classroom support in an Egyptian National Curriculum school preferred
