# Head of Section (Stage Principal) — Cambridge Curriculum

## Role Overview
Provides academic and operational leadership for a section (Primary, Lower Secondary, Upper Secondary, or Advanced) within a Cambridge International school. Reports to the Headmistress/Headmaster.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead curriculum delivery aligned to the Cambridge International curriculum for the section
- Manage and develop Heads of Department and teaching staff within the section
- Monitor student attainment and Cambridge assessment outcomes across the section
- Ensure compliance with Cambridge International School accreditation requirements
- Represent the section in Senior Leadership Team meetings and Cambridge moderation visits

## Required Skills
- Cambridge Curriculum
- Instructional Leadership
- Staff Performance Management
- Data Analysis
- Stakeholder Communication

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- Cambridge Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 6 years teaching with at least 3 years in a middle or senior leadership role
- Experience leading a section in a Cambridge International school required

## Stage Context
Specify stage: Cambridge Primary, Cambridge Lower Secondary, Cambridge Upper Secondary (IGCSE), Cambridge Advanced (A Level)
