# Education — Skill Vocabulary Reference

Use these exact phrases when writing required skills for education roles.
Candidates write these phrases on their CVs — use them verbatim to ensure screening matches.

## Curriculum Types
Use ONLY the curriculum name that matches the school. Never mix these:
- British schools: "British Curriculum" or "National Curriculum (England)"
- Cambridge schools: "Cambridge Curriculum" or "Cambridge International" or "CIE"
- American schools: "American Curriculum" or "Common Core"
- IB schools: "IB Curriculum" or "International Baccalaureate" or "IB MYP" or "IB DP"
- IGCSE: "IGCSE" or "International GCSE"

## Pedagogy Skills
- Use "Differentiated Instruction" (not "Differentiation")
- Use "Classroom Management" or "Behaviour Management"
- Use "Lesson Planning" or "Lesson Planning and Delivery"
- Use "Formative and Summative Assessment" or "Assessment"
- Use "Curriculum Planning" or "Curriculum Mapping"
- Use "Inquiry-Based Learning"
- Use "Special Educational Needs" or "SEN"

## Pastoral & Operations
- Use "Child Safeguarding" or "Safeguarding"
- Use "Parent Communication" or "Parent Liaison"

## EdTech
- Use "Educational Technology" or "EdTech" or "Technology Integration"
