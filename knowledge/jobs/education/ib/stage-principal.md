# PYP / MYP / DP Principal (Stage Principal) — IB Curriculum

## Role Overview
Provides academic and operational leadership for an IB programme (PYP, MYP, or DP) within an IB World School. Reports to the Head of School.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead curriculum planning and delivery aligned to the IB programme framework
- Manage and develop programme coordinators and teaching staff
- Monitor student achievement and inquiry-based learning outcomes across the programme
- Ensure compliance with IB authorization and evaluation requirements
- Represent the programme in Senior Leadership Team meetings and IB evaluation visits

## Required Skills
- IB Curriculum
- Instructional Leadership
- Staff Performance Management
- Data Analysis
- Stakeholder Communication

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- IB Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 6 years teaching with at least 3 years in a middle or senior leadership role
- Experience leading an IB programme in an IB World School required

## Programme Context
Specify programme: PYP (ages 3-12), MYP (ages 11-16), DP (ages 16-19)
