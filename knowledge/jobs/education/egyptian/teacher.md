# Teacher — Egyptian National Curriculum

## Role Overview
Delivers subject-specific lessons aligned to Egyptian Ministry of Education curriculum. Reports to Stage Supervisor (Nazir) or School Principal.

## Key Responsibilities
- Plan and deliver lessons per Egyptian Ministry of Education curriculum
- Assess students using Egyptian national assessment standards
- Maintain student records per Ministry requirements
- Participate in school inspection preparation
- Deliver Arabic and national subject content as required

## Required Skills
- Egyptian National Curriculum
- Arabic Language
- Lesson Planning
- Classroom Management
- Student Assessment

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- Egyptian National Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 2 years teaching in Egyptian national or language school
- Egyptian teaching license preferred
