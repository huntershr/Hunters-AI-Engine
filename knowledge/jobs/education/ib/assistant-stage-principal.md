# Vice Principal (Assistant Stage Principal) — IB Curriculum

## Role Overview
Supports the Stage Principal in the academic and operational leadership of an IB programme within an IB World School.

## Key Responsibilities
- Support the Principal in monitoring IB programme delivery and academic standards
- Assist in managing teaching staff and daily programme-level operations
- Support student welfare, behaviour management, and inquiry-based learning delivery
- Assist with IB authorization compliance and evaluation preparation
- Deputize for the Principal as required

## Required Skills
- IB Curriculum
- Instructional Leadership
- Behaviour Management
- Data Analysis
- Staff Support and Mentoring

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- IB Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 4 years teaching with prior leadership or coordination experience
- Experience in an IB World School required

## Programme Context
Specify programme: PYP (ages 3-12), MYP (ages 11-16), DP (ages 16-19)
