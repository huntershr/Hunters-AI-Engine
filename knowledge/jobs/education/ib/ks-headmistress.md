# Head of School — IB Curriculum

## Role Overview
Senior school leader with overall academic and operational responsibility across the school's IB programmes. Reports to the Board of Directors / Owner.

## Key Responsibilities
- Set and uphold academic standards and vision in line with the IB mission and philosophy
- Lead, appraise, and develop Programme Principals and senior staff across PYP, MYP, and DP
- Oversee IB authorization and evaluation readiness across all programmes
- Manage school budget, resourcing, and staffing plans
- Serve as primary point of contact for parents and the Board on school-wide matters

## Required Skills
- IB Curriculum
- Strategic Educational Leadership
- Staff Development
- Budget and Resource Management
- Stakeholder and Governance Relations

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- IB Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 8 years teaching with significant senior leadership experience
- Prior Head of School or Programme Principal experience in an IB World School required
