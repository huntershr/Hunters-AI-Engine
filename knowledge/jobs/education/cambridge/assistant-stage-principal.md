# Deputy Head of Section (Assistant Stage Principal) — Cambridge Curriculum

## Role Overview
Supports the Head of Section in the academic and operational leadership of a section within a Cambridge International school.

## Key Responsibilities
- Support the Head of Section in monitoring curriculum delivery and academic standards
- Assist in managing section teaching staff and daily operations
- Support student welfare, behaviour management, and Cambridge assessment preparation
- Assist with Cambridge accreditation compliance and moderation preparation
- Deputize for the Head of Section as required

## Required Skills
- Cambridge Curriculum
- Instructional Leadership
- Behaviour Management
- Data Analysis
- Staff Support and Mentoring

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- Cambridge Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 4 years teaching with prior leadership or coordination experience
- Experience in a Cambridge International school required

## Stage Context
Specify stage: Cambridge Primary, Cambridge Lower Secondary, Cambridge Upper Secondary (IGCSE), Cambridge Advanced (A Level)
