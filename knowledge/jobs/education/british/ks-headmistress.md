# KS Headmistress — British Curriculum

## Role Overview
Senior school leader with overall academic and operational responsibility for a Key Stage within a British Curriculum school. Reports to the School Principal / Director.

## Key Responsibilities
- Set and uphold academic standards and vision for the Key Stage in line with British Curriculum requirements
- Lead, appraise, and develop Stage Principals and senior teaching staff
- Oversee Key Stage compliance, inspection readiness, and accreditation standards
- Manage Key Stage budget, resourcing, and staffing plans
- Serve as primary point of contact for parents and governors on Key Stage matters

## Required Skills
- British Curriculum
- Strategic Educational Leadership
- Staff Development
- Budget and Resource Management
- Stakeholder and Governance Relations

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- British Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 8 years teaching with significant senior leadership experience
- Prior Headship or Deputy Headship experience in a British Curriculum school required

## Key Stage Context
Specify Key Stage: KS1 (Years 1-2), KS2 (Years 3-6), KS3 (Years 7-9), KS4 (Years 10-11), KS5 (Years 12-13)
