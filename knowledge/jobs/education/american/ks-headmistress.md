# Head of School — American Curriculum

## Role Overview
Senior school leader with overall academic and operational responsibility across the school within an American Curriculum school. Reports to the Board of Directors / Owner.

## Key Responsibilities
- Set and uphold academic standards and vision in line with American Curriculum and accreditation requirements
- Lead, appraise, and develop Principals and senior staff across school levels
- Oversee accreditation readiness and compliance across the school
- Manage school budget, resourcing, and staffing plans
- Serve as primary point of contact for parents and the Board on school-wide matters

## Required Skills
- American Curriculum
- Strategic Educational Leadership
- Staff Development
- Budget and Resource Management
- Stakeholder and Governance Relations

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- American Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 8 years teaching with significant senior leadership experience
- Prior Head of School or Principal experience in an American Curriculum school required
