# Stage Principal — British Curriculum

## Role Overview
Provides academic and pastoral leadership for a Key Stage (KS1-KS5) within a British Curriculum school. Reports to the Headmistress/Headmaster or Vice Principal.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead academic standards and curriculum delivery across the Key Stage
- Manage and develop Heads of Department and teaching staff within the stage
- Monitor student attainment, progress, and welfare data across the Key Stage
- Ensure compliance with British Curriculum / National Curriculum (England) requirements
- Represent the Key Stage in Senior Leadership Team meetings and school inspections

## Required Skills
- British Curriculum
- Instructional Leadership
- Staff Performance Management
- Data Analysis
- Stakeholder Communication

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- British Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 6 years teaching with at least 3 years in a middle or senior leadership role
- Experience leading a Key Stage in a British Curriculum school required

## Key Stage Context
Specify Key Stage: KS1 (Years 1-2), KS2 (Years 3-6), KS3 (Years 7-9), KS4 (Years 10-11), KS5 (Years 12-13)
