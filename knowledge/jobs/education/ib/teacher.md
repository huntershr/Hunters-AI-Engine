# Teacher — IB Curriculum

## Role Overview
Delivers subject-specific lessons aligned to International Baccalaureate (IB) framework. Reports to IB Coordinator or Head of Department.

## Key Responsibilities
- Plan and deliver lessons aligned to IB framework (PYP/MYP/DP)
- Implement inquiry-based learning central to IB pedagogy
- Assess using IB internal and external assessment criteria
- Contribute to IB programme review and self-study
- Collaborate with IB coordinator on programme delivery

## Required Skills
- IB Curriculum
- Inquiry-Based Learning
- Lesson Planning
- Classroom Management
- Differentiated Instruction

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- IB Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 2 years teaching in an IB World School
- IB category 1/2/3 training preferred

## Programme Context
Specify programme: PYP (ages 3-12), MYP (ages 11-16), DP (ages 16-19)
