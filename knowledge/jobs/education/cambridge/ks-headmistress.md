# Headmistress — Cambridge Curriculum

## Role Overview
Senior school leader with overall academic and operational responsibility across the school within a Cambridge International school. Reports to the Board of Directors / Owner.

## Key Responsibilities
- Set and uphold academic standards and vision in line with Cambridge International requirements
- Lead, appraise, and develop Heads of Section and senior staff across the school
- Oversee Cambridge International School accreditation and moderation readiness
- Manage school budget, resourcing, and staffing plans
- Serve as primary point of contact for parents and the Board on school-wide matters

## Required Skills
- Cambridge Curriculum
- Strategic Educational Leadership
- Staff Development
- Budget and Resource Management
- Stakeholder and Governance Relations

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- Cambridge Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 8 years teaching with significant senior leadership experience
- Prior Headship or Head of Section experience in a Cambridge International school required
