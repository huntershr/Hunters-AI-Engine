# Homeroom Teacher — IB Curriculum

## Role Overview
Generalist primary teacher responsible for delivering core subjects (English, Math, Science) to KG–Year 3 / Grade 1-3 students within an IB Curriculum school.

## Key Responsibilities
- Deliver core subjects (English, Math, Science) to primary/foundation stage students
- Create a nurturing and stimulating classroom environment
- Assess and report on student progress
- Communicate regularly with parents
- Maintain pastoral responsibility for homeroom class

## Required Skills
- IB Curriculum
- Classroom Management
- Lesson Planning
- Parent Communication
- Child Safeguarding

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- IB Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 2 years teaching primary/foundation stage in an IB Curriculum school

## Stage Context
KG through Year 3 (British) / Grade 3 (American) / PYP (IB) / Cambridge Primary
