# Head of Department — Egyptian National Curriculum

## Role Overview
Leads a subject department within an Egyptian National Curriculum school. Manages teachers, oversees curriculum delivery, and ensures academic standards.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead curriculum planning and delivery aligned to Egyptian National Curriculum standards
- Manage and mentor department teachers
- Monitor student progress and attainment data
- Coordinate with Senior Leadership Team on department performance
- Oversee department resources and budget

## Required Skills
- Egyptian National Curriculum
- Curriculum Leadership
- Teacher Mentoring
- Data Analysis
- Lesson Planning

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- Egyptian National Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 5 years teaching with at least 2 years in a leadership role
- Experience in Egyptian National Curriculum school required
