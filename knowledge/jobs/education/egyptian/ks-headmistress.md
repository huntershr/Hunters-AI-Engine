# School Principal / مدير المدرسة (KS Headmistress) — Egyptian National Curriculum

## Role Overview
Senior school leader with overall academic and operational responsibility across the school within an Egyptian National Curriculum school. Reports to the Board of Directors / Owner.

## Key Responsibilities
- Set and uphold academic standards and vision in line with Ministry of Education requirements
- Lead, appraise, and develop Stage Supervisors (Nazir) and senior staff across the school
- Oversee Ministry of Education compliance and inspection readiness school-wide
- Manage school budget, resourcing, and staffing plans
- Serve as primary point of contact for parents, the Board, and Ministry officials on school-wide matters

## Required Skills
- Egyptian National Curriculum
- Strategic Educational Leadership
- Staff Development
- Budget and Resource Management
- Stakeholder and Governance Relations

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- Egyptian National Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 8 years teaching with significant senior leadership experience
- Prior Principal or Stage Supervisor experience in an Egyptian national or language school required
