# Vice Principal (Assistant Stage Principal) — American Curriculum

## Role Overview
Supports the Stage Principal in the academic and operational leadership of a school level within an American Curriculum school.

## Key Responsibilities
- Support the Principal in monitoring curriculum delivery and academic standards
- Assist in managing teaching staff and daily school-level operations
- Support student discipline, welfare, and standards-based progress monitoring
- Assist with American accreditation compliance and reporting
- Deputize for the Principal as required

## Required Skills
- American Curriculum
- Instructional Leadership
- Behaviour Management
- Data Analysis
- Staff Support and Mentoring

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- American Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 4 years teaching with prior leadership or coordination experience
- Experience in an American Curriculum school required

## Grade Level Context
Specify grade level: KG, Grade 1-5 (Elementary), Grade 6-8 (Middle School), Grade 9-12 (High School)
