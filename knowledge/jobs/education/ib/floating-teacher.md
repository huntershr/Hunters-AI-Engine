# Floating Teacher / Cover Teacher — IB Curriculum

## Role Overview
Provides cover teaching across subjects and year groups within an IB Curriculum school. Ensures continuity of learning during teacher absences.

## Key Responsibilities
- Deliver cover lessons across subjects and year groups
- Follow lesson plans left by subject teachers
- Maintain classroom management and discipline
- Report back to HOD or cover coordinator after each lesson
- Adapt quickly to different subjects and year groups

## Required Skills
- IB Curriculum
- Classroom Management
- Lesson Planning
- Adaptability
- Behaviour Management

## Essential (Deal Breaker)
- IB Curriculum

## Experience
- Minimum 2 years teaching experience in an IB Curriculum school
